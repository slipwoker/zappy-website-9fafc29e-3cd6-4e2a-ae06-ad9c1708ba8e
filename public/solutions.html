Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products, services, and professional home security camera installation process — answered by our technical team.

01

Is SecuraHome suitable for professional installers as well as private homeowners?

Absolutely. SecuraHome is designed to serve both audiences with equal precision. Professional installers benefit from bulk pricing tiers, technical documentation, and direct importer access to brands like Provision-ISR, WiiM, and SpeakerCraft. Private customers receive the same premium equipment with straightforward guidance, curated bundles, and dedicated pre-sale support to ensure the right system for their home. Whether you are specifying a multi-site commercial deployment or upgrading a single residence, our catalog and team are built to support you.

02

Are your security and smart home systems compatible with each other?

Yes. Our product range is selected with interoperability as a core criterion. Security cameras, alarm systems, intercom and access control, and smart home automation equipment are chosen to integrate cleanly across IP-based infrastructure. Many of our solutions support standard protocols including ONVIF, Z-Wave, Zigbee, and major voice assistant ecosystems. Where specific compatibility questions arise — for example, pairing a Provision-ISR NVR with a third-party access control panel — our technical team can advise on verified configurations before you purchase.

03

What warranty coverage can I expect on products purchased from SecuraHome?

All products sold through SecuraHome carry the manufacturer's official warranty, which typically ranges from one to three years depending on brand and product category. As a direct importer, we are an authorised distribution point, which means warranty claims are handled efficiently without third-party delays. Extended warranty options are available on select product lines. Full warranty terms are listed on each product page, and our support team can clarify coverage specifics for any item in your cart before checkout.

04

What does your delivery process look like, and do you offer any installation support?

We ship nationwide with tracked courier services, and most in-stock items dispatch within one to two business days. Large or sensitive equipment — such as home theater racks, NVR units, or speaker systems — is packaged to professional standards with protective foam and double-boxing where required. While SecuraHome is primarily a supply and specification resource, we maintain a network of vetted installation partners and can connect you with a qualified low-voltage technician in your region. Reach out via our expert consultation channel to discuss installation requirements alongside your order.

05

How do I choose the right equipment for my specific security or smart home automation system needs?

Selecting the right system depends on factors including property size, existing infrastructure, resolution requirements, integration goals, and budget. Our product pages include detailed technical specifications, compatibility notes, and application guides to help you evaluate options independently. For more complex projects — such as a full smart home automation system with integrated CCTV, audio distribution, and network infrastructure — we strongly recommend using our expert consultation service. A member of our technical team will review your requirements and recommend a matched, future-proof solution at a competitive direct-import price.

06

Do you offer any support for network infrastructure planning alongside security installations?

Yes — network infrastructure is a core part of our offering. A reliable, well-structured network is the foundation of any modern security or smart home deployment. We stock managed switches, PoE injectors, structured cabling components, and wireless access points from trusted brands. Our team can advise on network topology for IP camera systems, NVR connectivity, and whole-home Wi-Fi coverage to ensure your security and automation systems perform without bottlenecks. This makes SecuraHome a single-source solution for the complete low-voltage stack.
